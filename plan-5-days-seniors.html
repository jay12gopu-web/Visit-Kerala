The Gentle Route

Two comfortable bases, no rushed hill roads.

Spend two nights in Kochi and two in Kumarakom. The route keeps transfers short, uses a daytime backwater cruise and leaves space to rest every afternoon.

Comfort and accessibility note

Confirm lift access, step-free rooms, bathroom supports, boat boarding steps and walking distances directly with each provider. Travellers with health concerns should check activity suitability with their doctor.

Day By Day

Your 5-Day Gentle Kerala Plan

Day01

Kochi Arrival + Rest

Use a pre-booked car from the airport or railway station, settle into the hotel and keep the day free except for a short sunset drive or early dinner.

  • Private arrival
  • Hotel rest
  • Optional sunset
Day02

Easy Kochi Heritage Day

Tour Fort Kochi by car with two short walking stops. Choose seated museum time, a long lunch and an optional evening Kathakali performance.

  • Short heritage stops
  • Long lunch break
  • Seated cultural show
Day03

Kochi To Kumarakom

Leave after breakfast for a comfortable road transfer to Vembanad Lake. Check in before lunch and keep the afternoon for rest, garden views and sunset.

  • Late departure
  • Lakeside check-in
  • Free afternoon
Day04

Slow Vembanad Backwaters

Take a two-hour covered motorboat or shikara cruise with easy boarding confirmed in advance. Return for lunch and a quiet resort afternoon.

  • Covered day cruise
  • Resort lunch
  • Restful evening
Day05

Leisurely Kochi Departure

Have an unhurried breakfast, check out and drive to Kochi with a planned comfort stop. Keep a generous margin for traffic and departure formalities.

  • Slow breakfast
  • Comfort stop
  • Departure buffer
