Before You Go

Travel Info

Helpful notes and direct Kerala Tourism links for planning a more realistic trip.

Best Route Logic

For short trips, start with Kochi and Alappuzha. For 5 to 7 days, add Munnar and Thekkady. For longer trips, add Wayanad, Kozhikode, Kannur, or Bekal.

What To Pack

Bring breathable clothes, sun protection, comfortable walking shoes, a light rain layer, swimwear for beach resorts, and modest clothing for temples or heritage sites.

Good For

Kerala works for families, couples, nature lovers, wellness breaks, food trips, slower holidays, and first-time India travelers who want variety in one route.
