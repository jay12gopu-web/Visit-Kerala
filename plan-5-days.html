The Route

Three landscapes, one smooth loop.

This route combines Kochi, Munnar, Thekkady, and Alappuzha. It suits travelers who want Kerala's greatest contrasts without packing every day too tightly.

KochiMunnarThekkadyAlappuzha

Day By Day

Your 5-Day Kerala Plan

Day01

Kochi Arrival

Arrive in Kochi, settle in, and choose a gentle Fort Kochi or Mattancherry heritage walk. Keep the first day light after travel.

  • Fort Kochi
  • Waterfront sunset
  • Local dinner
Day02

Into Munnar's Tea Country

Drive uphill to Munnar, stopping for viewpoints and waterfalls on the way. Spend the afternoon among tea estates and cool, misty scenery.

  • Tea estates
  • Viewpoints
  • Highland stay
Day03

Munnar to Thekkady

Continue to the Periyar-Thekkady region, known for spice plantations, lake country, and forest-based experiences. Add a guided plantation visit or nature walk.

  • Spice garden
  • Periyar region
  • Nature activity
Day04

Alappuzha by Houseboat

Travel down to Alappuzha and swap hills for canals. Board a houseboat for a relaxed afternoon, sunset, dinner, and night on the water.

  • Backwater cruise
  • Village scenery
  • Houseboat overnight
Day05

Back to Kochi

Enjoy breakfast as the boat returns to the jetty. Travel to Kochi for departure, adding a craft or spice stop if your flight or train timing is comfortable.

  • Morning cruise
  • Craft shopping
  • Departure
