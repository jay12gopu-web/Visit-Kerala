Quiet Islands & Waterways

For travellers who want to swap busy sightseeing for canoe rides, coconut groves, estuaries, village food, and long empty stretches of water.

Choose Your Pace

The quieter side of Kerala.

These places work best as one or two-night additions to a longer route. Go gently, use local guides where possible, and keep the schedule light enough to notice the water and village life.

  • Best with homestays, small boats and local food
  • Add only one island base to avoid long transfers

Kollam

Munroe Island

At the meeting of Ashtamudi Lake and the Kallada River, Munroe Island is ideal for narrow-canal boat rides, coconut groves, coir-making communities, and a stay that feels properly disconnected.

1-2 nights Canoe or small-boat ride Homestay feel
Near Kochi

Kadamakkudy Islands

A low-key cluster of islands north of Kochi, Kadamakkudy suits early-morning photography, paddy fields, fishing scenes, birdlife, and a slow drive or cycle away from the city.

Sunrise visit Easy from Kochi Landscape photography
Thiruvananthapuram

Poovar Island

Poovar is an estuary escape where river, backwater and sea meet. Choose it for boat approaches, a quiet beach mood, and a gentler southern Kerala finale before or after Varkala.

Estuary scenery Beach add-on Wellness-friendly
