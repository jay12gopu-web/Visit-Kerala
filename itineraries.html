3
Short Vacation

3-Day Kochi + Backwaters

Best when you have limited time and want Kerala's most recognizable mix: heritage streets and houseboat life.

Day 1

Arrive Kochi. Fort Kochi, Mattancherry, local cafes, evening Kathakali show.

Day 2

Drive to Alappuzha. Board a houseboat through canals, village scenery, and paddy fields.

Day 3

Breakfast on the backwaters, return to Kochi, shop for spices and crafts.

5
Balanced Escape

5-Day Munnar, Thekkady & Alappuzha

Best for first-time travelers who want hills, wildlife, spices, and a backwater cruise without rushing too hard.

Day 1

Kochi arrival and heritage walk.

Day 2

Munnar tea plantations, viewpoints, and cool highland stay.

Day 3

Thekkady or Periyar region for spice plantations and wildlife-style experiences.

Day 4

Alappuzha houseboat cruise.

Day 5

Return to Kochi for departure.

7
Recommended Classic + Offbeat

7-Day Islands, Hills + Coast

Best overall option: Kerala's famous hill route with Kadamakkudy and Munroe Island woven into the journey, followed by a Varkala finish.

Days 1-2

Fort Kochi heritage plus a sunrise visit to Kadamakkudy's island roads and wetlands.

Days 3-4

Munnar tea estates, valleys, plantation experiences, and highland viewpoints.

Day 5

Thekkady and Periyar for spice gardens, lake country, and soft adventure.

Day 6

Munroe Island homestay and a small-canoe journey through narrow village canals.

Day 7

Varkala cliff walk, beach or Ayurveda session, then departure.

10
Offbeat Deep Dive

10-Day Kerala North + South

Best for travellers who want the classic hills plus three quieter island experiences across south, central, and north Kerala.

Days 1-3

Kochi and Kadamakkudy, followed by a Munroe Island canoe and homestay night.

Days 4-6

Munnar and Thekkady for hills, tea, spices, and wildlife experiences.

Days 7-8

Wayanad forests, waterfalls, plantations, and caves.

Days 9-10

Valiyaparamba's island backwaters, Malabar food, and Bekal Fort before departure.
