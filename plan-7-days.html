The Route

The Kerala classics, with quieter islands built in.

This route keeps Kochi, Munnar and Thekkady, then trades the busiest backwater stop for Munroe Island. Kadamakkudy adds a slow village morning close to Kochi, while Varkala finishes the week by the sea.

Day By Day

Your 7-Day Kerala Plan

Days01-02

Kochi + Kadamakkudy Islands

Settle into Fort Kochi on Day 1. On Day 2, begin among Kadamakkudy's island roads, paddy fields and fishing villages before returning for Mattancherry and a Kathakali evening.

  • Fort Kochi
  • Kadamakkudy sunrise
  • Mattancherry + Kathakali
Days03-04

Munnar: Tea and Mist

Head into Munnar for two slower days. Let the weather shape the plan: tea estate walks, viewpoints, waterfalls, and sunrise lookouts all work best with a little flexibility.

  • Tea gardens
  • Viewpoints
  • Plantation stay
Day05

Thekkady: Spice Country

Travel to Thekkady for plantation landscapes and the Periyar area. Choose one gentle outdoor activity, then leave the evening open for a spice garden or local cooking experience.

  • Periyar region
  • Spice plantations
  • Forest views
Day06

Munroe Island: Small-Canal Kerala

Swap the busy houseboat corridor for a village homestay on Munroe Island. Take a small canoe through narrow palm-lined canals, meet local life at water level, and stay for the quiet evening.

  • Sunset canoe ride
  • Village homestay
  • Ashtamudi backwaters
Day07

Varkala Cliff + Beach Finish

Leave Munroe after breakfast for Varkala. Walk the cliff path, relax by the Arabian Sea, add a wellness session if time allows, then continue to Thiruvananthapuram for departure.

  • Varkala cliff walk
  • Beach or Ayurveda
  • Departure
