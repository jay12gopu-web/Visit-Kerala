The Route

Kerala's essential rhythm, with breathing room.

Give each part of the state enough time to land. This plan follows the classic south Kerala circuit and keeps the final day deliberately flexible.

Day By Day

Your 7-Day Kerala Plan

Days01-02

Kochi: Heritage and Culture

Spend a full day exploring Fort Kochi, Mattancherry, galleries, cafes, churches, and the waterfront. Keep the second evening for Kathakali or a local food experience.

  • Fort Kochi
  • Mattancherry Palace area
  • Kathakali
Days03-04

Munnar: Tea and Mist

Head into Munnar for two slower days. Let the weather shape the plan: tea estate walks, viewpoints, waterfalls, and sunrise lookouts all work best with a little flexibility.

  • Tea gardens
  • Viewpoints
  • Plantation stay
Day05

Thekkady: Spice Country

Travel to Thekkady for plantation landscapes and the Periyar area. Choose one gentle outdoor activity, then leave the evening open for a spice garden or local cooking experience.

  • Periyar region
  • Spice plantations
  • Forest views
Day06

Kumarakom or Alappuzha

Move to the backwaters for a houseboat stay or lakeside resort. Kumarakom is ideal for a quieter lagoon feel; Alappuzha suits travellers keen on the classic canal cruise.

  • Houseboat or resort
  • Backwater sunset
  • Village scenery
Day07

Beach or Wellness Finish

Finish on the coast at Varkala or Kovalam, or use the morning for an Ayurveda session before departure. Choose the airport or rail connection that fits your final stop.

  • Varkala or Kovalam
  • Ayurveda option
  • Departure
