The Student Route

Three bases, one affordable adventure.

Start with Kochi's heritage and food, spend two nights in Munnar's tea country, then finish beside Alappuzha's beach and backwaters.

Student stay note

Some hostels accept only guests aged 18 or above and may limit group size. School groups and travellers under 18 should book supervised accommodation directly.

Day By Day

Your 5-Day Student Plan

Day01

Kochi Culture + Food

Arrive, drop the bags and use ferry or local transport for Fort Kochi. Mix heritage streets, waterfront views, cafes and a pre-booked cultural show.

  • Fort Kochi walk
  • Street food
  • Kathakali option
Day02

Road Trip To Munnar

Leave early in a shared cab or pre-booked group vehicle. Use one or two waterfall and viewpoint stops before checking into a hostel or budget stay.

  • Scenic transfer
  • Waterfall stop
  • Hostel evening
Day03

Munnar Viewpoints + Tea

Choose a compact circuit based on weather: tea gardens, a museum or plantation experience, viewpoints and one short guided nature walk.

  • Tea country
  • Viewpoint circuit
  • Short guided walk
Day04

Backwaters + Alappuzha Beach

Travel down to Alappuzha for a shared shikara, canoe or local backwater trip. Check in near town or the beach and keep sunset free.

  • Shared boat ride
  • Village canals
  • Beach sunset
Day05

Local Morning + Kochi Return

Start slowly near the beach or canals, then return to Kochi by train, bus or shared vehicle. Add shopping only when the departure timing is comfortable.

  • Beach morning
  • Budget transfer
  • Departure
