What To Do

Kerala Experiences

The must-do side of Kerala: houseboats, hill stations, beaches, Ayurveda, wildlife, arts, cuisine, temples, palaces, and festivals.

Houseboat Cruises

Float through backwaters around Alappuzha and Kumarakom, with coconut groves, paddy fields, village life, and Kerala meals on board.

Hill Stations

Munnar, Thekkady, and Wayanad bring tea, spice plantations, trekking, viewpoints, forests, and cool-weather stays.

Beaches

Kovalam, Varkala, Marari, Bekal, and Muzhappilangad cover relaxed beach time, cliff views, resorts, and coastal food.

Ayurveda & Wellness

Kerala Tourism highlights Ayurveda as a signature Kerala experience, with treatments, yoga retreats, and wellness stays across the state.

Arts & Festivals

Watch Kathakali, Kutiyattam, Mohiniyattam, Theyyam, and seasonal festivals like Onam and Vishu.

Cuisine & Shopping

Try appam, puttu, seafood, sadya, Malabar snacks, spices, crafts, and local markets in Kochi and Kozhikode.
