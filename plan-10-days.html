The Route

Kerala from hidden southern canals to northern islands.

This plan threads Kadamakkudy and Munroe Island into the classic hills, then travels north through Wayanad to Valiyaparamba's quieter backwaters and a final sunset at Bekal.

Day By Day

Your 10-Day Kerala Plan

Days01-02

Kochi + Kadamakkudy Islands

Open with Fort Kochi's heritage and food. On Day 2, visit Kadamakkudy at sunrise for island roads, wetlands and fishing life, then return for Mattancherry and an evening performance.

  • Fort Kochi
  • Kadamakkudy sunrise
  • Culture and food
Day03

Munroe Island Canoe Country

Travel south to a quieter backwater cluster. Check into a homestay, explore the narrow canals in a small canoe, and watch village life unfold where the Kallada River meets Ashtamudi Lake.

  • Small-canal canoe
  • Village homestay
  • Backwater sunset
Days04-05

Munnar High Country

Spend two nights in Munnar. Explore tea plantations, viewpoint roads, falls, and quiet valley walks without needing to hurry between activities.

  • Tea gardens
  • Hillside viewpoints
  • Plantation walks
Day06

Thekkady and Spices

Shift to the Periyar area. Choose a spice garden visit, a guided nature activity, or a lakeside experience depending on weather and local availability.

  • Periyar region
  • Spice gardens
  • Nature activity
Days07-08

Wayanad: Forests and Falls

Head north to Wayanad for a different Kerala. Plan for forests, plantation roads, waterfalls, caves, and a slower two-night highland reset.

  • Waterfalls
  • Plantations
  • Forest stay
Days09-10

Valiyaparamba Islands + Bekal

Finish in north Kerala with a boat or canoe through Valiyaparamba's island backwaters, local Malabar food, and a final coastal evening near Bekal Fort before departure.

  • Valiyaparamba cruise
  • Island village life
  • Bekal Fort sunset
