The Route

Culture, canals, and a quiet finish.

Ideal for a long weekend or a quick first visit. Start in Kochi's historic waterfront neighbourhoods, then continue south to Alappuzha for Kerala's signature backwater experience.

Day By Day

Your 3-Day Kerala Plan

Day01

Arrive in Kochi

Settle in and wander Fort Kochi's colonial lanes. Visit the Chinese fishing nets, St. Francis Church, and the Mattancherry area; finish with a Kathakali performance if timings allow.

  • Fort Kochi walk
  • Mattancherry
  • Kathakali evening
Day02

Alappuzha Houseboat

Drive to Alappuzha and board a traditional-style houseboat. Glide through canals, paddy fields, and village stretches, then enjoy sunset and dinner on the water.

  • Scenic transfer
  • Houseboat cruise
  • Backwater sunset
Day03

Breakfast on the Water

Wake to a slow breakfast on the backwaters. Disembark after the morning cruise, return to Kochi, and allow time for spices, crafts, or a final waterfront coffee before departure.

  • Morning cruise
  • Spice shopping
  • Kochi departure
